High Performance Computing

Standard and high security HPC to run your code, generally written in R and Python.

HIPAA Compliance

For patient data or other secure applications requiring HIPAA, we have multiple options on-grounds and in the AWS cloud.


Cloud

Have an idea you'd like to test? Need an environment provisioned in short-order? We can help you build in the AWS cloud.

Visualization

Sometimes numbers on a page, or data in a table, isn't enough to show others the significance of your research. UVA SOM has a visualization lab to help you show your data.


Storage

Need large, or extremely large storage offsite or on grounds? Can you count in GB, TB, or PB? Learn more about storage options and pricing.


Data Transfer

Sometimes you have the right data, but in the wrong place. There are several paths available for researchers depending upon the size and destination of your data.
